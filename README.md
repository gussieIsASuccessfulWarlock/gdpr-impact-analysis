# GDPR Impact Analysis

A comprehensive numerical analysis examining the impact of GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and other EU digital regulations on various metrics including internet usage, cloud adoption, R&D investment, and digital infrastructure across Germany, Ireland, and Switzerland from 2010-2025.

## Overview

This project analyzes the effects of major EU regulatory milestones on digital transformation metrics:
- **GDPR** (May 25, 2018)
- **DSA** - Digital Services Act (November 16, 2022)
- **DMA** - Digital Markets Act (May 2, 2023)
- **AI Act** (February 2, 2025)

The analysis includes 41 visualizations covering:
- Broadband pricing and infrastructure evolution
- Internet usage adoption rates
- Individual and enterprise cloud service adoption
- R&D expenditure trends (GERD, BRED, GOVERD, HRED)
- Social media platform compliance monitoring
- Technology usage by European firms
- Cost impacts of EU regulations on businesses
